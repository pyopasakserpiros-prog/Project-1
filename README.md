Jianghu RPG 🗡️
Jianghu RPG is a browser-based, text-driven martial arts (Wuxia) RPG. Designed with a focus on deep character progression, strategic decision-making, and a transparent numerical system. Experience the journey from a novice disciple to a legendary Grandmaster of the Jianghu.
🚀 Core Design Philosophy
Progressive Growth: Feel stronger every session with a clear advancement path.
Strategic Depth: Customize your character with specialized builds (STR, AGI, INT) and unique skill combinations.
Data-Driven Architecture: Built upon a robust schema that ensures balance and scalability without compromising the core experience.
Browser-Ready: Zero installation required—optimized for instant access.
🛠 Key Features
Character & Advancement
Rank System: Climb through 9 tiers of martial arts mastery, from Novice to Grandmaster.
Free-Form Builds: Allocate stat points freely every level to define your combat style.
Advancement Milestones: Earn significant bonuses and unlock new slots every 10 levels.
Combat System
Sequential Turn-Based: Strategic combat where speed, accuracy, and armor penetration determine the outcome.
Skill Synergy: Mix and match Active and Passive skills to create powerful combos.
Follower AI: Manage up to 3 followers to fight alongside you, each with their own AI behavior and rarity-based abilities.
Progression & Loot
Rarity Framework: 6 levels of rarity (Common to Mythic) applied to items, skills, and followers.
Dynamic World: Explore 10 levels of maps, each featuring unique enemies, mini-bosses, and grand Area Bosses.
Event System: Immerse yourself in the world with dynamic events that test your luck and choices.
⚖️ Balanced Mechanics
Hard Caps: All critical stats (CRIT, DODGE, LIFE_STEAL, etc.) are governed by strict caps to maintain long-term game balance.
Scaling Difficulty: Enemy stats scale dynamically based on map levels, ensuring a consistent challenge throughout the end-game.
Fair Economy: A single gold-based economy with built-in "sinks" to prevent inflation.
💻 Technical Overview
This project is built using a Data-Driven Architecture, allowing for seamless expansion of content (maps, skills, enemies) via standardized JSON schemas without altering the core game engine.
Core Systems: Battle Engine, Stat Pool, Advancement, and Exploration.
Data Models: Standardized schemas for Players, Items, Skills, Enemies, and Events.
